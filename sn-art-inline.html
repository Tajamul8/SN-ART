The Spirit Of Kashmir

SN ART was born out of a deep reverence for the architectural lines, lake reflections, and historical artisan lanes of Srinagar. Our mission is to bridge the gap between generational master craftsmen and discerning patrons of luxury across the globe.

Each creation in our catalogue is catalogued with its unique artisan lineage and GI certification (Geographical Indication). By bringing a piece of SN ART into your home, you support the preservation of ancient skills, ensuring these rich craft forms flourish for generations to come.

Terms & Conditions

Welcome to SN ART. By exploring our catalog and placing inquiries or orders, you agree to follow the terms below, which are designed to support and protect our community of master artisans in Jammu & Kashmir.

1. Artisan Craft & Authenticity

SN ART guarantees that all catalog items are authentic, certified products sourced directly from local Kashmiri artisan families. Items labeled with "GI-Tagged" are certified Geographical Indications verifying authentic regional heritage.

2. Handmade Variations

Because our creations—including hand-spun Pashmina shawls, lacquer papier-mâché, hand-carved walnut wood, and willow tokri baskets—are entirely made by hand using traditional looms and chisels, minor variations in pattern, texture, size, and coloring are natural. These differences are not flaws, but rather the unique signature of individual craftsmanship.

3. Custom Orders & Commissions

Custom commissions (such as personalized wood carvings, special shawl weaving codes, or size modifications) require a 50% advance deposit. Once production begins, custom commissions cannot be cancelled or refunded, as raw materials are explicitly acquired for the artisan's labor.

4. Shipping, Transit & Insurance

We provide secure shipping across India, with free shipping on orders above ₹2,500. Due to the handcrafted nature and intensive labor of some products, delivery timelines may vary. All high-value shipments include full transit insurance coverage.

5. Returns & Handcrafted Care

Standard items can be returned or exchanged within 14 days of delivery, provided they are in their original, unused condition. Customers are responsible for reviewing and adhering to the specific care guides provided (e.g. strict dry clean instructions for Pashmina wool, avoiding dry heat/moisture for walnut wood).

6. Governing Law & Jurisdiction

These terms and conditions are governed by the laws of India. Any legal disputes or claims arising out of transactions with SN ART shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir.

For custom requirements or support, contact our concierge desk at concierge@snart.in.
